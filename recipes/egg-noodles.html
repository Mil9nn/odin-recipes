Egg Noodles

Easy Egg Nooldes HomeMade Pasta Recipe

These egg noodles are the old-fashioned type, just like Grammy used to make! Try them next time you prepare your favorite soup or stew.

Ingredients

  • 2 Â½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 large eggs beaten

Steps:

  1. Stir flour and salt together in a large bowl. Add eggs, milk, and butter.
  2. Knead dough until smooth, about 5 minutes. Let rest in a covered bowl for 10 minutes.
  3. On a floured surface, roll dough out to a thickness of 1/8 to 1/4 inch. Cut into desired lengths and shapes.
  4. Cook noodles in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente, about 5 minutes.
