Shredded Brussels Sprouts with Bacon and Pine Nuts

Description

These shredded Brussels sprouts with bacon and pine nuts taste fantastic! After eating this dish, my family now requests I bring this dish to every get-together!

Ingredients

  • ½ pound sliced bacon
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ⅔ cup pine nuts
  • 2 pounds Brussels sprouts, cored and shredded
  • 3 green onions, minced

Steps:

  1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until crisp. Drain, reserving 2 tablespoons of grease in the skillet, crumble and set aside.
  2. Melt butter with reserved bacon grease in the same skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add pine nuts; cook and stir until browned. Add Brussels sprouts and green onions; season with seasoning salt and pepper.
  4. Cook over medium heat until sprouts are wilted and tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Stir in crumbled bacon just before serving.
